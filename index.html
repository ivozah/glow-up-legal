30-Day Glow-Up: Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This page covers both the Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use for the 30-Day Glow-Up app ("the app", "we", "us"). If you have any questions, you can reach us through the "Send feedback" option in the app or via the contact details on our App Store listing.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is the point of this app. We built it local-first: your skincare data lives on your phone, not on our servers.

Your selfies never leave your device

The photos you take in the app are stored privately on your device only. They are kept in the app's own private storage, which is not accessible to other apps and is not your camera roll. We do not upload them, back them up to any cloud, send them to any server, or share them with anyone. We cannot see your photos. No one can, unless you choose to share an image yourself using your phone's share sheet.

The app applies visual filters only (soft glow, blur, tone). It never reshapes your face and never uses any kind of AI or generative editing.

Your scores and routine data stay on your device

Your Glass Skin Score, your daily routine check-offs, your streak, your name, and your progress history are all stored locally on your device. This is the source of truth for the app and it does not leave your phone.

What we do collect

Anonymous usage analytics. To understand how people use the app and where the experience can improve, the app may send anonymous product-analytics events to PostHog, our analytics provider. These events describe actions in the app (for example: an onboarding step was completed, a routine item was checked, a score was set). They do not include your photos, and they are not used to identify you personally. Analytics is configured at build time and can be absent entirely, in which case the app sends nothing.

Support emails you choose to send. If you tap "Send feedback" in the You tab, your email app opens a message addressed to us. If you send it, we receive your email address and whatever you write, so we can reply and improve the app. This only happens when you choose to send it.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect or upload your photos.
  • We do not collect your precise location.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or ad tracking.
  • We do not require an account to use the app.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your choices and rights

Because your personal skincare data lives on your device, you are in control of it. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or deleting the app, which removes the local database and your stored selfies from your device. For anything related to the limited data described above (analytics, support emails), you can contact us through the "Send feedback" option in the app or via the contact details on our App Store listing to ask what we hold or to request deletion.

Changes to this policy

If we change what data the app collects (for example, if we later add optional cloud backup or accounts), we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above before that change ships. Optional cloud features, if added, will be off by default and clearly explained.
